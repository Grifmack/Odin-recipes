chicken carbonara pasta bake

This chicken carbonara pasta bake makes a great Sunday dinner for the whole family. Serve it with roasted veggies, a fresh salad, and crusty bread to soak up all the extra sauce.

Ingredients

  • Pancetta
  • One 16-ounce package of spaghetti.
  • Four egg yolks.
  • Two cups of Parmesan cheese.
  • Heavy cream - make sure it is room temperature.
  • Cooked chicken breast.
  • Fresh Italian parsley.

How to cook the chicken breast

  1. Heat a skillet over medium heat.
  2. Add one tablespoon of oil and the chicken breast.
  3. Cook for about 16 minutes, flipping halfway, until an instant read thermometer registers 170 degrees F.

How to Cook our Chicken Carbonara Pasta Bake

  1. Cook the pancetta until lightly browned and crisp, then set aside.
  2. Boil spaghetti, reserve two cups of pasta water, and set aside.
  3. Whisk the eggs in a bowl, then whisk in 1 Â½ cups of the hot pasta water.
  4. Whisk in the cheese and cream.
  5. Add the pasta, pancetta, and chicken to the sauce and toss.
  6. Transfer the mixture to a prepared baking dish and bake until the sauce is thickened.
  7. Stir and let stand so the sauce can continue to thicken. Add more pasta water if you like.
  8. Garnish with parsley and Parmesan cheese.
